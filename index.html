Welcome!

Welcome to "All About Filmmaking"! We will talk about the equipment used to make movies, the pre-production and post-production processes, and techniques to use while filming. These will only be the broad strokes for the craft of filmmaking, but they will help you better construct your movies. You can always do more research on certain topics that you enjoy or need more explaining.
